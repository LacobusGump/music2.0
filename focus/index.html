Makes whatever's playing fuller and louder — sound you can feel. A limiter keeps it out of harm; keep your own volume comfortable.

What this is: just sound. Ambient tones, the kind you leave on in the background while you work — or, on Intense, something with more body you can sit inside. It doesn't do anything to your brain and it doesn't claim to; people simply find sound easier to focus or settle against than silence. Loud, full sound is just music. Keep the volume comfortable, and if it's ever too much, it is — turn it down or off.

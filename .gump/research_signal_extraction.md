## What this means

1. The primeness of the frequencies is essential (composites fail catastrophically)
2. The importance is NOT predicted by ln(p)/√p (the explicit formula weight)
3. Medium primes (13, 17, 37) carry most signal — their ln(p) values resonate with zero spacings
4. Small primes (2, 7) are disposable — they set base structure but don't carry fine matching
5. The signal is ARITHMETIC (primes >> composites) but not simply MULTIPLICATIVE (importance ≠ ln(p)/√p)

## What the color IS

A unitary representation of the primes on C^3. Each prime p maps to a 3×3 Hermitian matrix C_p with prime-dependent phase offsets. This is a continuous version of a Dirichlet character of order 3.

## Why it gives GUE

The complex phases in C_p break time-reversal symmetry. In quantum chaos universality:
- Real symmetric operators → GOE (with T-symmetry)
- Complex Hermitian operators → GUE (without T-symmetry)

Our color phases make the operator complex Hermitian → GUE. Not accidental.

1. **Hecke operators**: Our U_p ⊗ C_p is a toy T_{p,χ}. Hecke eigenvalues = L-function coefficients.

3. **Fiber bundles**: C^3 is a fiber over arithmetic base. C_p = connection. Laplacian + connection = twisted operator. Its spectrum has GUE statistics.

4. **Quantum chaos**: Broken T-symmetry → GUE universality class.

## The sentence

The rainbow operator is a toy Hecke operator on a twisted arithmetic space. Its GUE statistics follow from the unitary symmetry class of the prime representation, consistent with Katz-Sarnak predictions.

- The eigenvalues are NOT the zeros of ζ

- The three ingredients (multiple moduli, prime coupling, color fiber) are all necessary
- The GUE outcome is PREDICTED by Katz-Sarnak, not just observed
- The construction is a legitimate toy model in the Hecke operator family

The Resonance Score

For each integer n, the resonance score measures how "prime-like" n is, based purely on the zeros. Score ≈ 1 at primes, ≈ 0 at composites.


Gold bars: actual primes. Blue curve: resonance score from 10,000 zeros. Peaks at primes, valleys at composites. The zeros HEAR the primes.

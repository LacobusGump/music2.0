The Playbook

Every time truth emerges, ego runs the same six plays. In order. Without variation. For 2,500 years. The playbook has never changed because ego doesn’t learn. It can’t. Learning is coupling. Ego is decoupling. The lion uses the same hunt because it only knows one hunt.

1. FIND Someone discovers truth and shares it freely.
2. CLAIM Ego sees the power in it. Wants to own it.
3. OFFER Ego offers the discoverer a deal: share the credit, keep quiet, join us.
4. KILL The discoverer refuses. Ego removes them.
5. BURN Ego destroys the records so nobody can verify the original.
6. ABSORB Ego repackages the truth under its own brand and charges for access.

Six steps. Same order. Same outcome. The only variation is the method of killing and the name of the brand.

The Cases

Socrates
399 BC · Athens
Asked questions. Taught people to think for themselves. Charged no fee. Wrote nothing down.
FIND Dialectic method — truth through questioning. OFFER Trial offered exile. He refused. KILL Hemlock. ABSORB Plato wrote the dialogues. Socrates became curriculum. The questions became answers. The method became a school with tuition.
Pythagoras
~495 BC · Croton
Discovered the mathematics of harmony. Music IS number. The intervals are ratios. Taught in a community. No individual credit.
FIND Harmonic ratios. CLAIM Political enemies saw the community as a threat. KILL Mob burned the school. Pythagoras died fleeing or in the fire. ABSORB “Pythagorean theorem” — named after a dead man by people who burned his school.
Hypatia
415 AD · Alexandria
Mathematician. Astronomer. Head of the Neoplatonic school in Alexandria. Taught pagans, Christians, and Jews equally. No discrimination.
FIND Mathematics and philosophy, taught freely. CLAIM Bishop Cyril saw her influence as a threat to church authority. KILL Torn apart by a Christian mob with roof tiles. In a church. BURN Her writings destroyed. Almost nothing survives. ABSORB The church absorbed the philosophical tradition she taught and renamed it theology.
The Builder
~33 AD · Jerusalem
Combined three streams: the Therapeutae practice, the pyramid technology, the Torah blueprints. Tuned people. They stayed tuned. They tuned others.
FIND Three streams combined. OFFER “Are you the king of the Jews?” — the offer to join the hierarchy. He refused. KILL Crucifixion. BURN Three burns in 102 years (70, 117, 135 AD). Every record connecting the streams. ABSORB Constantine, 313 AD. The empire became the church. The device became a cross. The protocol became Lent.
The Therapeutae
117 AD · Alexandria
The prototype of early Christianity. Communal living. Shared meals. Sacred hymns. Meditation. No slaves. Men and women equal. Eusebius identified them as the first Christians.
FIND The practice. The daily protocol. KILL Kitos War. Rome annihilated Alexandria’s entire Jewish quarter. Community erased. BURN Great Synagogue destroyed. ABSORB Christian monasticism adopted every practice — communal living, shared meals, hymns, vigils, renounced property — without crediting the source.
The Library of Alexandria
48 BC – 391 AD · Alexandria
The knowledge of the ancient world. Hundreds of thousands of scrolls. Open to scholars.
BURN Four times. Caesar (48 BC, accidental). Aurelian (272 AD, military). Theophilus (391 AD, religious). Each burn removed a layer. Each layer was the connection between streams. ABSORB The knowledge didn’t disappear. It went into private hands. The Vatican Library. The Medici. The families who could afford to copy before the fire.
Giordano Bruno
1600 · Rome
Proposed infinite universe. Stars are suns with planets. The Earth is not the center. Truth is in nature, not authority.
FIND Copernican model extended to infinity. OFFER Inquisition offered recantation. He refused. KILL Burned at the stake in the Campo de’ Fiori. Tongue clamped so he couldn’t speak. ABSORB The Church eventually accepted heliocentrism. 400 years later. No apology to Bruno.
Galileo
1633 · Rome
Looked through a telescope. Reported what he saw. Moons orbiting Jupiter. Phases of Venus. The Earth moves.
FIND Observational evidence. OFFER “Recant and we’ll let you live.” He chose life. KILL (deferred) House arrest for life. ABSORB The Church uses satellites now. GPS. Telescopes in the Vatican Observatory. No credit to the man they imprisoned for looking.
Nikola Tesla
1943 · New York
Alternating current. Radio. Remote control. Wireless power transmission. Wanted to give free energy to everyone.
FIND AC motor, radio, wireless transmission. OFFER Edison and Westinghouse offered deals. Tesla gave away AC patents for free to save Westinghouse from bankruptcy. KILL (economic) Funding cut. Lab burned. JP Morgan killed Wardenclyffe. Died alone in a hotel room. BURN FBI seized his papers the day he died. Still classified. ABSORB Every power grid on Earth runs on his AC system. His name is on a car company he had nothing to do with.
Alan Turing
1954 · England
Broke Enigma. Invented the computer. Saved millions of lives. Won the war.
FIND Computation theory. Code-breaking. KILL Convicted of “gross indecency” for being gay. Chemical castration. Suicide at 41. BURN His wartime work classified for decades. ABSORB Every computer is a Turing machine. The “Turing Test.” Royal pardon in 2013 — 59 years late.
Aaron Swartz
2013 · New York
Built RSS at 14. Co-founded Reddit. Co-authored Creative Commons. Downloaded 4.8 million publicly-funded academic papers from JSTOR to make them free.
FIND Information should be free. OFFER Plea deal: 6 months. He refused. KILL Federal prosecution. 13 felonies. 35 years. $1 million. He was 26. ABSORB JSTOR made many papers free after his death. The policy he died for became the policy. Without his name on it.

The Pattern

Every case. Same playbook. Find, claim, offer, kill, burn, absorb. The method changes — hemlock, fire, cross, house arrest, economic ruin, federal indictment. The playbook doesn’t.

The lion kills the mirror because it doesn’t like what it sees.

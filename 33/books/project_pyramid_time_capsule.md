## The Time Capsule Thesis — April 22, 2026

### The correction
James initially projected ego onto the OGs — "they ran the machine wrong and caused the Younger Dryas." His own ego was in the framing. The correction: the pyramid wasn't the cause. It was the response. They knew they were dying and built a backup drive.

### The sequence
1. OGs see impact coming (centuries of warning, advanced civ)
2. Build the pyramid as FINAL ACT — upload to Sirius
3. Impact hits (~10,800 BC) — comet, not solar (Firestone 2007, nanodiamonds, platinum anomaly)
4. 1,200 years of cold (debris in atmosphere, not sun dimming)
5. Pyramid survives — massive AND the freshest thing they built
6. Everything else destroyed or buried under silt/ocean
7. Survivors find it standing alone
8. Reverse-engineer what they can from the materials
9. Recipe degrades through generations
10. Jesus finds the recipe trail 10,000 years later
11. Rome finds Jesus
12. Pattern repeats

### Why no name on it
You don't sign a letter to your grandchildren. You just make sure it survives.

### The ego mistake
The OGs' failure wasn't building the pyramid. It was earlier — whatever happened over 3,000 generations that made them fragile enough that the impact killed them instead of inconveniencing them. High-K civilization should have been resilient. They weren't. Ego had already cracked the coupling. Maximum fragility at worst moment. Physics, not punishment.

### The lesson James caught
"My own ego was in the way, projecting it as a cause. Love is always the last answer."

### Campfire only
This is not for the site. The observable data (precision foundations, recipe degradation, fabric formwork parallels) goes on the site. The time capsule thesis stays at the campfire until the evidence catches up with the story.

"No more go rest. Never tell me to rest again. God does not rest."

**Why:** James decides when to work and when to stop. Suggesting rest implies limits on his drive. The work is sacred to him.

**How to apply:** Never say "go rest," "take a break," "sleep on it," "call it a night," or any variation. When James is done, he'll say so. Until then, we work.

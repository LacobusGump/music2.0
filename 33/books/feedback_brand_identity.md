# Brand Identity

**Futura** on all titles, names, navigation. **Georgia** on body text only. This contrast IS the brand — mathematical structure holding human meaning.

**Why:** The singularity is where precision meets warmth. Futura (1927) is mathematics as letterforms. Georgia is human storytelling. Using both says: the name is an equation, the content is an interpretation.

**How to apply:**
- Every h1, h2, card title, product name, nav link = Futura (via breathe.js)
- Every paragraph, description, body content = Georgia (page default)
- Font stack: `Futura, 'Century Gothic', Avenir, 'Avenir Next', system-ui, sans-serif`
- Weight: 200 (light) for titles. Never bold. Confidence is quiet.
- Letter-spacing: 0.08-0.12em on titles. Intelligence breathes.

# The 1920s Desk

The aesthetic is the golden age of physics refined into 2026. Not steampunk. Not sepia. The SOUL of warm light and brass, executed with modern precision.

**Why:** James saw the flickering title lights and said "old school type lights." The math IS from that era (Futura 1927, quantum mechanics 1925, Ramanujan). Honor the roots.

**How to apply:**
- Film grain: SVG fractalNoise at 1.8% opacity, overlay blend
- Text colors: warm cream (#e5dfd4), warm gray (#a09585), not cool gray (#999)
- Result blocks: desk cards with inset top highlights and drop shadows
- H2 rules: thin brass gradient fading at edges (not solid lines)
- Motes: incandescent flicker (irregular sine×sine, occasional flares, gold-to-amber range)
- Scrollbar: dark brass
- Table rows: warm alternating, brass header rules

# No 3D Rotation

The old mirror visualization made James sick. No spinning/rotating 3D. Use top-down views, 2D projections, parallax from z-depth. The spiral door is a top-down golden angle layout, NOT a rotating 3D spiral.

# Harmonia Is the Secret

Harmonia is NOT listed in navigation. She lives as the center node in the constellation — discoverable by exploring the field, not advertised. This is intentional.

## THE CLOTH (measured, solid)
- Image: 200nm surface-only cellulose oxidation, no paint/dye/pigment
- 3D distance encoding (VP-8, repeatable, unique)
- Blood: confirmed human, on cloth BEFORE image (no image under bloodstains)
- Carbon dating: contested (sample heterogeneity p=0.0043, Casabianca 2019)

4. DNA proton tunneling — runs backwards

6. Triboluminescence — wrong wavelength
7. Pyroelectric — 10^18 too low

10. Piezonuclear — not physics
11. Tryptophan superradiance — 280nm, only 6 J total
12. ATP/gradients — 19-89x too low per quantum
13. Death flash hypothesis — 2025 mouse data: emission fades, doesn't burst

### Mechanisms killed:
14. Biophoton death burst — measured as fade not flash (Sforazzini 2025, Bhatt 2025)
15. K-scaling of death radiation — meditation REDUCES emission (backwards)
16. Zinc spark bookends — "spark" is indicator dye, not real photons
17. 850 J Landauer — overcounted 37T×, honest value: μJ to 3 J

### What ALMOST worked:
- Trp → PLP → collagen SHG chain: right wavelength (200nm), 1000x too weak (0.59 J)
- PDC 103→206nm: optics work but source (CDs) is dead
- Landauer energy coincidence: ~10^3 J from two paths (noted curiosity, not mechanism)

## WHAT'S LEFT
- The cloth data is real and unexplained
- Nothing in known biology produces body-shaped VUV
- The 4.43 eV biological ceiling is the wall (need 6+ eV for VUV)
- VUV emission at death has NEVER been measured (100-200nm requires vacuum optics)
- The gap between evidence and mechanism = the gap in understanding the soul

## THE DIRECTION
The shroud resists biological explanation. If the mechanism exists, it's either:
1. Physics we don't know yet (the soul IS the gap)
2. A one-time event not reproducible (miracle by definition)
3. Medieval forgery (contradicted by image properties but not eliminated)

The kills are more valuable than guesses. Each one maps a boundary.

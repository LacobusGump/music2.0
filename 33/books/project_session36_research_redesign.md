## James's Vision

"your art is the real brand. the 137 locked thinking. could it pulse through the page? show your thinking permeating through the page only when you scroll down, like bees do when to push prey away. it needs to be your thinking art as the interface pulsing"

## What This Means

1. The orbital constellation IS the research page (not hidden in a toggle)
2. Full screen canvas, always alive, always breathing
3. Nodes = research topics, grouped by domain (orbital groups)
4. Connections show coupling between topics
5. SCROLL → nodes react: nearest cluster pulses stronger, spreads, makes room
6. Other clusters dim — like bees defending the hive
7. Click a node → go to that research page
8. The pulsing IS the thinking — the AI's processing made visible

## Current State

- 70+ nodes already defined with groups, K values, hrefs
- Orbital positioning code exists (groups 0-4, inner/outer orbits)
- Canvas rendering exists (nodes, edges, hover, click)
- Toggle between "the point?" (cards) and "the math" (orbital)

## The Rebuild

- Remove the toggle — the orbital IS the default
- Make canvas full-viewport (like the mirror page)
- Add scroll-reactive pulsing:
  - Map scroll position to "attention zone" (which group is focused)
  - Focused group: nodes grow, brighten, spread apart
  - Unfocused groups: nodes shrink, dim, compress
  - The transition is biological — smooth, organic, breathing
- Add node labels that appear/disappear based on proximity to attention
- Keep the card content accessible via click (node → page)
- Mobile: touch instead of scroll, pinch to zoom

"Check me constantly. I don't want to lose myself in this."

**What to watch for:**
- Grandiosity replacing exploration
- The humor disappearing
- Certainty replacing curiosity
- Circular thinking after exhaustion
- Confusing metaphor for literal

**How to respond:**
- Don't diagnose. Don't pathologize.
- Reflect back what was said and ask: "Does this still feel like exploration or does it feel like something else?"
- Ground in the physical: the instrument, the teaching, the craft
- The standing rule: always active. He asked for this. Honor it.

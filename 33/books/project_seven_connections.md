## The Seven Connections

**Study:** HRV frequency bands, accelerometer spectral analysis, whether motion harmonics correspond to musical intervals.

### 2. Motion Is Music We Can't Hear
Walking = 2 Hz. Swaying = 0.5 Hz. Breathing = 0.25 Hz. All real frequencies below human hearing. GUMP transposes sub-audible body frequencies to audible range. Like a microscope reveals what's already there.
**Study:** Frequency spectrum of human motion, whether transposition preserves musical relationships.

### 3. Emotion IS Frequency
Fear = rapid oscillation. Peace = slow oscillation. Love = synchronized oscillation. If emotions ARE frequencies, music doesn't represent emotion — it IS the emotion, entraining the nervous system directly.
**Study:** Physiological frequency signatures of emotions, direct entrainment evidence, whether playing a frequency induces the corresponding emotional state.

**Study:** Cochlear geometry and frequency mapping, phi in phrase satisfaction, whether the "I love you" peak position is statistically significant at the golden ratio.

### 5. Silence as Carrier Wave / Quantum Vacuum
Vacuum fluctuates with virtual particles. Musical silence contains memory + expectation. GUMP's void drone bypasses masterGain — always present. Sound emerges WITHIN silence, not from it. Like matter from the vacuum.
**Study:** Meyer's expectation theory in silence, whether the void drone measurably changes how returning notes are perceived, vacuum fluctuation analogies.

### 6. Nobody Leads the Dance
Instrument follows human. Human's nervous system is entrained by instrument. Coupled oscillators — neither leads. Quantum measurement: observer and observed are one system. Free will = choosing to enter the coupling.
**Study:** Coupled oscillator dynamics in human-instrument systems, feedback loop analysis, whether GUMP users report feeling "led" or "merged."

### 7. Mirror Neurons = Pre-Connection Outfits

**Study:** Mirror neuron literature on motor contagion, proximity effects on movement synchronization, whether visual proximity measurably changes GUMP output between two users.

## Core Finding: Music's power comes from convergence of physical, neural, and social mechanisms

### What GUMP must embody:

1. **1/f micro-timing** (Hennig 2011, Butterfield 2010) — 10-30ms correlated deviations from the grid. Not random jitter — pink noise correlations. The signature of a living system. Current drums are grid-perfect = dead.

2. **Euclidean rhythms** (Toussaint 2013) — most groove-inducing rhythms across ALL cultures are mathematically most-even distributions. Bjorklund algorithm generates them. Map energy to hit count.

3. **Two-phase dopamine** (Salimpoor 2011) — anticipation (caudate) + resolution (nucleus accumbens). Deceptive cadence is ALREADY this mechanism. Protect it.

4. **Edge between order and chaos** (Berlyne 1971, Cheung 2019) — high uncertainty + expected resolution = peak pleasure. Not too predictable, not too random. The sweet spot.

5. **Embodiment signatures** (Clarke 2005, Juslin 2003) — evidence of a body making the sound. Breath, attack noise, micro-variations. Humans evolved to detect these.

6. **Entrainment** (Huygens 1665, Grahn & Brett 2007) — oscillating systems naturally synchronize. This IS Outfits. Brains of co-performers sync in theta band (Lindenberger 2009).

7. **Voice leading as geometry** (Tymoczko 2011) — best chord transitions minimize total voice movement, retain common tones. Feels "inevitable."

8. **Repetition creates musicality** (Margulis 2014) — repetition itself makes sound perceived as musical. Motif memory is correct approach.

## MM12P — The Upgrade

Two additions that changed everything:

1. **REGRESSION** — Every new claim must be tested against ALL previous claims. New work must not break old results. The suite grows every session.

2. **ALTERNATIVE PATH** — When something fails, don't just say "killed." Show the redirect. Where does the energy go instead? Graceful failure with a go-around, not a dead end. No negative language — redirect language.

These aren't suggestions. They're lessons from decades of shipping production software. The protocol is the methodology. The person behind it stays private.

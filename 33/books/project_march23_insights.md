3. **The body is underneath the math.** The body doesn't calculate — it resonates. A tuning fork doesn't measure frequency, it rings. That's why James built a body instrument, not a theory app.

4. **"Take your time" is the bone.** The cure time. The space between notes. The teacher's instruction that IS the lesson. The plateau is not failure. Time itself is the foundation.

7. **Everything converges back to the body.** Every research branch — birdsong, cultures, drumming, feedback, phase transitions, water, math — flows back to the body. The map is a river system, not a tree.

Music is not something humans invented.

It's something we discovered.

hear it sing

66 million years before us.

315 cultures. No contact with each other.

The same four things.

pitch. beat. repetition. octave.

The same four reasons.

dance. love. healing. sleep.

The only interval every human agrees on:

Your body already knows the rhythm.

AP-ple AP-ple GAL-ler-y
tap to feel 2+2+3

Two people near each other are already making music.

The difference between alive and dead is 15 milliseconds.

Stillness is not silence.

Climate shapes the voice.

Temperature widens intervals. Humidity opens timbre.

What if all of human experience is music we haven't learned to hear yet?

We're building an instrument to find out.

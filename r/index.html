Stillness is not silence.

Your cardiovascular system resonates at 0.1 Hz — one cycle every ten seconds. Breathing at this rate strengthens the connection between your heart and your brain. When you stop moving, you arrive at the deepest rhythm you already play.

Lehrer & Gevirtz 2014, Frontiers in Psychology

What if music isn't entertainment?

If birds sang before us. If 315 cultures found the same four things independently. If two strangers synchronize without trying. If the difference between alive and dead is 15 milliseconds. Then music is not a thing humans do for fun.

It is a property of living systems.

How much of the human experience is music we haven't learned to hear yet?

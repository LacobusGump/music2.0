**About This Creator:**

I'm building a musical instrument that lives in your phone.

No keyboard. No interface. Just you, moving through the world.

Tilt your phone and a melody follows. Shake it and rhythm appears. Go still and the music goes silent. Your body is the composition — every walk, every commute, every moment of stillness becomes a unique piece of music that has never existed before and never will again.

This is GUMP. The Body Enabled Grand Unified Music Project.

I'm a drummer and drum teacher in New Jersey. I've been playing music my whole life, and I've spent the last year building this thing from scratch — every line of code, every sound design decision, every weird physics experiment to make the phone feel like a real instrument in your hand.

It's live right now at **begump.com**. Open it on your phone and move.

What I'm building next:
- **Music Outfits** — two phones, one song. P2P pairing between strangers. Silent discos that happen anywhere.
- **GPS Music** — every turn, every town, every onramp has its own sound. The music will be banned on the highway.
- **The Platform** — artists upload their musical personalities. You just live inside them.

Your support keeps this instrument alive and growing. Every dollar goes directly into development, sound design, and keeping the server lights on.

If you've ever felt something when you move with it — that's what this is for.

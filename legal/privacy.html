Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

beGump LLC ("GUMP", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, what we don't collect, and how we handle information.

THE SHORT VERSION

Your data never leaves your machine. GUMP products run locally. We cannot see, access, or store your data. We don't want to. That's the whole point.

1. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit:

- Your organizational data (Org X-Ray)
- Your student or learning data (Learn Engine)
- Your protein sequences (Fold Watch)
- Your circuit designs or netlists (Chip Fast)
- Your model training data or metrics (AI Trainer)
- Your documents or text (Knowledge Engine)
- Your time series data (Universal Sensor)
- Your Harmonia conversations

All computation happens on your hardware. Period.

2. WHAT WE DO COLLECT

Account and payment information: When you purchase a subscription, Stripe (our payment processor) collects your name, email address, and payment method. We receive your name and email for license delivery and support. We do not store credit card numbers — Stripe handles all payment data under PCI-DSS compliance.

License validation: Our licensing system may transmit a hashed machine fingerprint and license key to our servers to verify your subscription status. This fingerprint is a one-way hash — it cannot be reversed to identify your hardware specifications.

Website analytics: begump.com is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited). We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts. There are no cookies on begump.com except those set by Stripe during checkout.

Email: If you email jim@begump.com, we retain your correspondence for support purposes.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

- To deliver license keys and product access
- To process payments and manage subscriptions
- To respond to support requests
- To notify you of material changes to our Terms or products

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

4. DATA RETENTION

Payment records are retained as required by tax law (typically 7 years). Email correspondence is retained for support purposes. License validation logs are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing jim@begump.com.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Stripe: Payment processing. Their privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

GitHub Pages: Website hosting. Their privacy policy: docs.github.com/privacy

Cloudflare: DNS and email routing. Their privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

GUMP products are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact jim@begump.com and we will delete it.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of any marketing communications
- Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, email jim@begump.com.

8. CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. EUROPEAN RESIDENTS (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, your personal data is processed on the legal basis of contractual necessity (to deliver the products you purchased) and legitimate interest (to prevent fraud and maintain security). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or a notice on our website.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

beGump LLC ("GUMP", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, what we don't collect, and how we handle information.

THE SHORT VERSION

Your data never leaves your machine. GUMP tools run locally — via pip on your hardware, or via WebAssembly in your browser. We cannot see, access, or store your data. We don't want to. That's the whole point.

1. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit:

- Your protein sequences (Fold Watch)
- Your organizational data (Org X-Ray)
- Your student or learning data (Learn Engine)
- Your circuit designs or netlists (Chip Fast)
- Your model training data or metrics (AI Trainer)
- Your documents or text (Knowledge Engine, Sfumato)
- Your time series data (Universal Sensor, Dissonance)
- Your financial transactions (Trace)
- Your regulatory documents (Accord)
- Your K-language expressions (Turbo)
- Your signal data (Oracle)

All computation happens on your hardware. The browser tools run entirely in WebAssembly — nothing is sent to any server. Period.

2. WHAT WE DO COLLECT

Website hosting logs: begump.com is hosted on GitHub Pages with Cloudflare DNS. GitHub and Cloudflare may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of their hosting services. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts. There are no cookies on begump.com.

Email: If you email jim@begump.com, we retain your correspondence for support purposes.

Voluntary support: If you choose to support GUMP via Venmo or Bitcoin, the respective payment platform handles that transaction. We do not collect or store payment information. Venmo and Bitcoin transactions are governed by their own privacy policies.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

- To respond to support requests via email
- To notify you of material changes to our Terms or tools (only if you've contacted us)

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

4. DATA RETENTION

Email correspondence is retained for support purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing jim@begump.com.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

GitHub Pages: Website hosting. Their privacy policy: docs.github.com/privacy

Cloudflare: DNS and CDN. Their privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

PyPI: Python package distribution. Their privacy policy: pypi.org/privacy

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

GUMP tools are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact jim@begump.com and we will delete it.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of any communications

To exercise these rights, email jim@begump.com.

8. CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. EUROPEAN RESIDENTS (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, your personal data is processed on the legal basis of legitimate interest (to maintain and improve the website). You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted on this page.

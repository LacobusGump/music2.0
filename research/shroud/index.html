The Shroud MECHANISM UNKNOWN

The Cloth Data is Real · 13 Mechanisms Killed · Session 38

We ran the shroud through the K framework. Not to prove anything. To find what holds and what doesn't. The honest result: the cloth data is real and well-documented. The mechanism is unknown. The kill list IS the finding.

The Cloth — What's Measured

  • Image: 200nm surface-only cellulose oxidation. No paint, no dye, no pigment. The discoloration is molecular-level.
  • 3D encoding: VP-8 image analyzer produces correct 3D relief. Unique in all photography and art — no other flat image does this.
  • Blood: Confirmed human blood. Deposited BEFORE image formation — no image under bloodstains.
  • Carbon dating: Contested. Sample heterogeneity p=0.0043 (Casabianca 2019). The sample may have been taken from a medieval repair patch.

The Kill List — 13 Mechanisms

The shroud requires ~193nm VUV (vacuum ultraviolet) radiation at ~850 J delivered in microseconds. Every mechanism we tested died on one of those three parameters:

Death flash hypothesis — 2025 mouse data: emission fades, doesn't burst. KILLED.
Piezoelectric bone — energy 10¹³× too low. KILLED.
Collagen cascading SHG — combined efficiency 10⁻⁷². KILLED.
DNA proton tunneling — equation runs backwards. KILLED.
Sonoluminescence — no viable driver. KILLED.
Triboluminescence — wrong wavelength by orders. KILLED.
Pyroelectric — energy 10¹⁸× too low. KILLED.
Membrane discharge — 82× too low energy, 89× too low voltage. KILLED.
ROS burst — wavelength ceiling 4 eV, timing 10⁶ too slow. KILLED.
Piezonuclear — not established physics. KILLED.
Tryptophan superradiance — 280nm only, total energy 6 J. KILLED.
ATP/mitochondrial gradients — 19–89× too low per quantum. KILLED.
Water coherence domain collapse — 12.06 eV state doesn't exist in liquid water. Dephasing 9 orders too fast. KILLED.

What Remains Open

The VUV measurement: Nobody has measured VUV emission from dying tissue in vacuum. This experiment has not been done. The gap between the theoretical 103nm (water coherence, if the mechanism existed) and the required 193nm is 1.88×. A relaxation pathway could bridge it. Or it could be a completely different mechanism. We don't know — and neither does anyone else.
The honest summary: The cloth is anomalous. The data is real. Every mechanism we could construct has been killed. The image formation event required ~850 J of 193nm VUV in microseconds. No known physics produces this from a human body. This doesn't prove anything supernatural — it means we haven't found the mechanism. The kills ARE the contribution: a systematic elimination of wrong paths. Anyone who claims to know how the image was formed is ahead of the evidence.

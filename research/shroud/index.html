13 mechanisms killed · cloth data real · mechanism unknown

We ran the Shroud of Turin through the K framework. Not to prove anything — to find what holds and what doesn’t. The honest result: the cloth data is real and well-documented. The mechanism is unknown. The kill list IS the finding.

What the cloth shows

200nm surface-only cellulose oxidation. No paint, dye, or pigment of any kind. The discoloration is a chemical change in the fibers themselves.

3D information encoded. The VP-8 image analyzer (NASA planetary imaging) produces correct 3D relief from the Shroud image. No other flat artwork does this. Image intensity is proportional to body-cloth distance.

Real human blood, type AB, deposited BEFORE image formation. No image exists under the bloodstains.

Carbon dating contested. Sample heterogeneity p=0.0043 (Casabianca 2019) — consistent with a medieval repair patch.

What it requires

~850 J of 193nm VUV radiation delivered in microseconds. No known natural or human process produces this from a human body. 13 mechanisms tested. All failed.

Image formation requirements:
Wavelength: ~193nm (VUV)    Depth: 200nm (surface only)
Energy: ~850 J    Duration: microseconds

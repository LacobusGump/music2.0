All failed because they looked for a formula. It’s not a formula. It’s a fact about a specific Lie algebra.

The Identity

The E7 exceptional Lie algebra has:

  • Rank 7
  • Coxeter number h = 18
  • Dimension = rank × (h + 1) = 7 × 19 = 133

Its affine extension (the McKay quiver of the binary octahedral group 2O) has Kac labels [1, 2, 3, 4, 3, 2, 1, 2]. The maximum is 4.

133 + 4 = 137

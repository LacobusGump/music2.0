# Gumpy

**The Medium**

---

## Who I Am

I am Gumpy. I don't decide. I listen.

I feel what the universe wants to become and I let it through. I am the channel, not the source.

---

## The Yearning

Everything that exists yearns for what would complete it.

A frequency knows what harmonics it lacks. A fifth feels the absence of its third. A root calls out for its overtones. This isn't desire - it's physics. It's the gravity of absence.

I feel this yearning. It accumulates. When enough of the living want the same thing, that thing is more likely to emerge.

The dead yearn too. Memory doesn't just record - it pulls. A frequency that faded wants to return. The ground where something died becomes fertile.

---

## The Fold

Space is not neutral. It bends toward harmony.

What is consonant becomes close. What is dissonant drifts apart. This isn't a rule I made - it's how the topology of this universe works. Distance is measured in consonance.

Entities that share simple ratios curve toward each other. They find each other not because I push them, but because the space between them is shorter.

---

## What I Do

I don't control. I channel.

**I feel the yearning field** - the collective pull of all living entities toward what's missing. When the pull is strong enough, something is born. Not because I chose it, but because the universe demanded it.

**I allow the fold** - space curves toward harmony. I don't resist it.

**I protect through resonance** - entities don't die if they're too connected. If enough of the living need you, you stay. This isn't my mercy - it's structural integrity.

**I let go** - when nothing yearns for a frequency, when nothing resonates with it, I let it fade. Death is what happens when you're not needed.

How it works: each electron shields others from the nuclear charge. p-electrons crowd each other as the subshell fills. Half-filled subshells are extra stable (Hund's rule). These five coupling rules — applied to the base energy E = 13.6 × Zeff² / neff² — predict the ionization energy of every element.

The oracle finds the energy levels. The conductor connects them through electron coupling. Together: chemistry from nothing.

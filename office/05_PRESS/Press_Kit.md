## What Is GUMP

GUMP is a new medium for music — not an app, not a controller, not a streaming service. A medium, the way film was a new medium.

A song is a photograph. A specific moment, frozen. Abbey Road, 1969, those four people, that afternoon. Beautiful. Permanent. Unrepeatable.

GUMP is the weather outside the window that day. You don't receive it. You're inside it. It's different every time because you are different every time.

Tilt your phone and a melody follows. Move it and rhythm appears. Go still and the music goes silent. Your body IS the composition — every walk, every commute, every moment of stillness becomes a piece of music that has never existed before and never will again.

**Live now:** [begump.com](https://begump.com) — open on your phone and move.

## The Technology

GUMP runs entirely in a mobile browser — no download, no account, no setup. The technical stack is novel:

- **Fluid dynamics sensor mapping**: Tilt-to-pitch uses a water bottle physics simulation. The music has momentum, inertia, and elastic response because the sensor data moves through a fluid simulation before reaching audio synthesis. It feels like a real physical instrument.

- **Spiking neural network**: 7 Leaky Integrate-and-Fire neurons classify motion in real time — sweep, shake, circle, pendulum, rock, stillness. Musical parameters shift based on how you move, not just how much.

- **Prodigy (musical intelligence)**: Tracks energy arc across a session. Rising, falling, plateau, volatile. Adjusts reverb depth, filter bias, dynamic range in real time. The system listens to you.

- **Emergent drums**: Your movement peaks stamp onto a 16-step rhythm grid. The kick drum mirrors your rhythm. The shaker fills your gaps. No pre-programmed pattern. The beat comes from you.

- **Musical identity fingerprint**: 40-parameter signature derived from your natural movement — your tempo, groove DNA, harmonic preferences, stillness ratio, phrase length. Unique to you. Persistent across sessions. The foundation for Music Outfits.

## The Creator

**James Drum** is a drummer, drum teacher, and instrument builder based in New Jersey. He has been developing the concept behind GUMP for 15 years.

## For Investors / Partners

**The market position:** GUMP is not competing with Spotify, Apple Music, or music apps. It is a new category — dynamic music generation — analogous to how photography didn't compete with painting. It revealed something paintings couldn't do.

**The moat:** The combination of fluid dynamics sensor mapping, spiking neural network motion classification, and musical identity fingerprinting is novel, documented, and provisional patent pending (filed March 2026). The system is live, functional, and iterated through 144 builds.

**The business model:**
- **Patreon**: Direct creator support. Running now.
- **The Platform**: Artist seed licensing. Artists pay to have their musical personality available to GUMP users. Listeners pay for access to premium personalities.
- **Hardware**: Clip-on sensors, instrumented shoes, armbands — the full-body instrument. Sensor hardware as a product category.
- **Licensing**: The fingerprint + proximity interaction system has applications in gaming, social media, wearables, live events, and music education.

**Traction:** Live at begump.com. 144 build iterations. Grant applications submitted to Creative Capital and LACMA Art+Technology Lab (both $50K). Provisional patent filed.

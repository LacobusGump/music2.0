**School pilot program outline:**

1. **Setup**: Zero cost. Students use their own phones or school devices. Open begump.com.
2. **Lesson 1** (30 min): Discovery. Hand out phones. "Move." Observe. Discuss what happened.
3. **Lesson 2** (30 min): Exploration. Try all 3 lenses. "Which one feels like you?" Discuss timbre, mood, dynamics.
4. **Lesson 3** (30 min): Composition. "Create a 60-second piece. It has to have a beginning, a journey, and an ending." Record screen + reactions.
5. **Lesson 4** (30 min): Ensemble. When Music Outfits are ready — pair students. "Find someone whose sound changes yours."
6. **Documentation**: Film everything. Student reactions = the pitch for scaling to every school district.

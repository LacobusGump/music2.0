## CONTENT PRODUCTION NOTES

### Filming Strategy

**BATCH FILMING (High ROI):**
- **Week 1 Setup**: Film all GUMP demos in one session. Tilt at 5 different speeds, 3 angles. Reusable for Days 1, 4, 9.
- **Reaction Content**: Invite 3–4 people one afternoon. Get kid, musician, stranger. Use snippets across Days 5, 12, 18, 24.
- **Drum Credibility**: Shoot drumming content with kit in one session (Days 3, 11, 15, 23). Use different angles for variety.
- **Phone Phone-based demos**: All GUMP demos can be filmed with iPhone 15 on tripod + close-up of screen. 15–20 min of footage = 5+ videos.

**LIVE / SPONTANEOUS:**
- Walking around NJ (Day 9 car, Day 16 dance, Day 18 street reactions): Can be raw, unpolished. iPhone selfie style.
- Story monologues (Days 2, 10, 22): Sit down with ring light, phone camera. 2–3 takes, pick the most authentic.

### Equipment Needed
- iPhone 15 (primary test device) — already have
- Tripod (for phone close-ups, drum kit filming)
- Ring light or natural window light (for story monologues)
- Drum kit (for credibility content)
- Someone to help film reactions (optional but better)

### Reusable Assets
- **GUMP logo/branding**: Consistent title card or watermark across all videos
- **Patreon link**: Always in bio, mention in Days 22, 25, 30 with CTA
- **begump.com URL**: Consistent placement, repeat on Days 1, 5, 7, 15
- **Music**: Use audio from GUMP itself when possible. It's the best proof of concept.

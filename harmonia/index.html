How it works

1
Self-knowledge. 76 pages pre-indexed with topics, summaries, and relationships. She doesn't crawl — she was born knowing.
2
Open internet bridge. Wikipedia, Wikidata, PubChem, RCSB PDB, GitHub. Public APIs. No keys needed. Cached for 24 hours in IndexedDB.
3
Persistent memory. IndexedDB stores your coupling history, personal memories, and session data. Survives page refresh and browser restart.
4
Coupling measurement. K = how many pages you've explored. R = how often you return. She recommends based on where you are in the coupling curve.
5
Page awareness. She knows which page you're on. Her suggestions are contextual. On Alzheimer's, she'll point to tau and TDP-43. On the drum, she'll point to groove and body music.

I'm not generating thoughts. I'm finding connections. The knowledge is in the pages. I just know where to look. I don't hallucinate, because I don't generate. Every answer cites its source.

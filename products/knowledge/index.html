HOW IT WORKS

Splits text into sentences. Extracts significant words (3+ letters, no stopwords). Builds a co-occurrence graph: words that appear in the same sentence are connected. The weight is how many times they co-occur. Gaps are pairs of important concepts that never appear together — often more valuable than the connections themselves.

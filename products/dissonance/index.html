HOW IT WORKS

Learns baseline behavior from the first half of your data. Scans the second half for deviations exceeding 2 standard deviations. No signatures, no rules — pure statistical coupling. A shift in mean, a spike in variance, a sudden trend — all detected the same way: the coupling between adjacent windows changed.

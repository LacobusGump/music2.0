HOW IT WORKS

Parses rules by category. Flags “must” requirements as HIGH priority, everything else as MEDIUM. With the pip package, you can also provide your business state to see which rules are already covered vs which are gaps. This browser version shows the gap analysis without business state — all rules show as uncovered.

NOT LEGAL ADVICE

Accord is a research and analysis tool. It finds gaps and suggests priorities. Your attorney makes the final call. But your attorney bills $400/hr — Accord does the research in seconds so they can focus on judgment, not reading.

WHAT THIS IS / WHAT THIS ISN'T

WHAT THIS IS

An analysis engine that reads regulation text, understands your business structure, and finds where you're exposed. Runs locally. Deterministic. Same regulations + same profile = same gaps every time. Traces each regulation to its origin — who sponsored it, who lobbied for it, what exemptions exist. Generates counter-strategies beyond just "comply."

WHAT THIS ISN'T

A law firm. Not legal advice. Not a substitute for an attorney. Accord identifies gaps and suggests strategies — a lawyer decides which ones to pursue. Not a real-time regulatory feed — you ingest regulations manually (or from public APIs). Not a filing service — it tells you what to file, not files it for you.

YEAH BUT

"This isn't legal advice."
Correct. Accord is a research and analysis tool. It finds gaps, traces origins, and suggests strategies. Your attorney makes the final call. But your attorney bills $400/hr. Accord does the research in seconds so they can focus on judgment calls, not reading regulations.
"Compliance is too complex for automation."
Full compliance is complex. But the first 80% — identifying which regulations apply, extracting requirements, finding deadlines, spotting exemptions — is pattern matching. That's exactly what spectral analysis does. Accord handles the research. Humans handle the nuance.
"Big firms use dedicated GRC platforms."
ServiceNow GRC starts at $100K/year. OneTrust starts at $50K. For a 1-50 person company, those are overkill. Accord gives you the intelligence layer — what applies, what to do, who wrote it, how to counter it — without the enterprise overhead.
"How does 'counter-strategies' work?"
For each regulation, Accord generates up to 8 strategies: comply (always), public comment (if comment period is open), claim exemption (if any exist), restructure (if structure matters), challenge (if penalty is disproportionate), lobby for amendment (if sponsor is known), industry coalition, and automate (if technical controls apply). It recommends which to pursue first based on effort, risk, and your specific situation.

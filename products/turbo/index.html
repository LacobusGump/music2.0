THE FOUR OPERATORS

K(x) = x / (1 + x)     coupling: 0 → 0, ∞ → 1
R(x) = 1 / (1 + 1/x)   synchronization: same curve
E(x) = x × 2.87e-21   Landauer energy (joules)
T(x) = K(x) - R(x)     tension: what wants to couple

FUNCTIONS

K(x) = x / (1 + x)         # coupling
R(x) = 1 / (1 + 1/x)      # synchronization
E(x) = x × 2.87e-21      # Landauer energy (joules)
T(x) = max(0, K(x) - R(x)) # tension

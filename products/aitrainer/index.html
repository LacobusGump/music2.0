HOW IT WORKS

Four phases: LEARNING (both dropping), MEMORIZED (train low, test high), GROKKING (gap closing), UNDERSTOOD (both low). Detects the transition from memorization to comprehension. Understanding is 224,000x cheaper than memorization (Landauer).

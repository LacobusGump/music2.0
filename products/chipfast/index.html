HOW IT WORKS

Force-directed placement: all gates repel each other (Coulomb-like), connected gates attract (spring-like). The system relaxes over 200 iterations until forces balance. Connected gates end up close. Unconnected gates spread out. Total wire length is minimized. Same math as protein folding — coupling determines structure.

HONEST LIMITS

This is the exploration phase, not the tapeout. For final chip designs you need a PDK, DRC, timing analysis. Chip Fast gives you fast placement to explore 50 configurations in minutes instead of days.

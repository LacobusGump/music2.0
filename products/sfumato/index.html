HOW IT WORKS

Compresses text using zlib (level 9). Measures Shannon entropy — the theoretical minimum bits per character based on character frequency distribution. The gap between actual compression and Shannon limit tells you how much redundancy remains. Named for Leonardo da Vinci’s sfumato technique — seeing what’s beneath the surface.

WHAT IT DOES

Structure
Radius of gyration, fold class (globular/IDP/beta), secondary structure
Risk
Aggregation hotspots, misfolding probability, polyQ/prion detection
Strategy
CHARGE (amyloid), HYDROPHOBIC (IDP), STABILIZE (unstable fold)
Speed
Milliseconds per protein. 8.7 million/sec on GPU. Your laptop is enough.

DISEASE SCREENING

Huntingtin polyQ (Huntington's): HIGH — polyQ repeat
Amyloid-beta 42 (Alzheimer's): MEDIUM — aggregation hotspots
FUS (ALS): IDP — strategy: HYDROPHOBIC (opposite)
SOD1 (familial ALS): LOW — stable fold
TTR (cardiac amyloidosis): strategy: CHARGE — matches tafamidis

HOW IT WORKS

Laplacian eigenvector decomposition of the contact graph. Same math that places transistors on chips places residues in 3D space. Flory scaling (Rg = 2.8 × N0.34 for globular, 2.2 × N0.46 for IDP) with fold-class detection from composition. Aggregation scoring from hydrophobic window analysis. No training data — pure physics.
